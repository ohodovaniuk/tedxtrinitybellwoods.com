What is TEDxTrinityBellwoods?

Our team has embraced the challenge of bringing the TEDx experience to communities in Toronto. We believe our cityâ€™s greatest assets include the ideas and dreams of its citizenry. Ideas have the power to reshape minds and change behaviour. But too often, dreams are deferred and ideas unheard, because they lack a platform that gives them voice. We want TEDxTrinityBellwoods to be that platform. Our goal is to present talks on issues that matter to our local community and have global impact, free from corporate, political, and religious agenda, and to spark a real dialogue about how to make a difference. TEDxTrinityBellwoods aims to connect people, spread ideas and inspire positive impact. We believe in the beauty of our city and its many diverse voices. Our goal is to ensure that those voices are heard and shared.
